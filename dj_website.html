About Me

"The noblest art is that of making others happy."

- P.T. Barnum


Hey guys! My name is Stephen Ray Guedea, aka DJ Stevie G, I was born and raised in San Antonio, TX, home of the San Antonio Spurs, Go Spurs Go! I've been a DJ since 1999, started helping my dad set up at events, then just started messing around with my dad's records and CDs in the garage and became fascinated with you can do with turntables and a mixer. Eventaully I started playing behind the tables for family functions at my grandma's backyard. One thing lead to another and next thing you know, a hobby become a career!

I've played and hosted numerous events, both private and public, and around the Southern Texan region, including San Antonio, Corpus Christi, Eagle Pass, Uvalde, Boerne, Fredericksberg, Hunt, Pearsall, just to name a few. Performed at public events such as Fiesta's Texas Cavaliers River Parade, Pride Center NYE Celebration, Pride Center Easter Picnic, Final Four Pre-game Party, again just to name a few. But no matter how big or small the crowd is, I'm determined to make sure that everyone will have a great time. It's your party, I'm just the DJ!
